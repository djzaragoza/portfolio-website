Medium

I am an active writer for Medium, publishing various articles, including "Life @LambdaSchool."

Why write for Medium?

I fell in love with the site several years ago when i read my very first article about Javascript. I did not know what JavaScript was until I kept reading the article. It was so interesting, my eyes were super glued to my phone.

There are a lot of technology sites, such as Hacker Noon, Tech Crunch, and Reddit, just to name a few, that provides thousands of articles about technology, programming, development, the latest news on programming languages, artificial intelligence and virtual reality. Medium provides a place to publish your best work. The internet should reward quality thinking, not just a place for users to do happy-clicking. Medium is a place for writers, journalists, experts and technologist, free from ads, and connects the end-users/readers to curious, avid readers, so you can focus on what matters: putting your best work about technology out there.
